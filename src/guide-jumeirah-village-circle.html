Jumeirah Village Circle

JUMEIRAH VILLAGE CIRCLE - DUBAI’S URBAN OASIS

Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC) is a thoughtfully planned residential community developed by Nakheel, offering a unique blend of urban convenience and suburban tranquility. Located along Sheikh Zayed Road, this expansive development spans multiple sub-districts, each with its own distinct character.

JVC stands out as a green community, featuring beautifully landscaped parks, walkways, and open spaces. This urban village concept provides residents with a peaceful environment away from the hustle and bustle of central Dubai, while still offering easy access to essential amenities and services.

The community’s design focuses on creating a family-friendly atmosphere, with a mix of apartments, townhouses, and villas to cater to diverse lifestyle needs. JVC’s strategic location and affordable housing options have made it increasingly popular among both renters and buyers seeking a balance between city living and suburban comfort.

LOCATION AND LANDMARKS

Jumeirah Village Circle is strategically positioned along Sheikh Zayed Road, providing excellent connectivity to other parts of Dubai. The community is divided into various sub-districts, including Districts 11, 12, 13, and others, each offering its own unique features and residential clusters.

  • Key landmarks include:
    • JVC Community Park: A central green space for recreation and relaxation.
    • Circle Mall: A community shopping center offering retail and dining options.
    • JSS International School: Catering the educational needs of the community.
    • Mediclinic Parkview Hospital: Providing healthcare to residents.
    • Al Khail Avenue: Am upcoming mall project set to enhance the area’s retail offerings

PROPERTY INSIGHTS

Jumeirah Village Circle offers a diverse range of property types, catering to various lifestyles with a mix of apartments, townhouses, and villas. Notable projects in the area include Ghalia Constella and Kensington Manor, popular for their studio and one-bedroom apartments; Le Grand Chateau, known for its attractive studio units in District 10; and Astoria Residence, featuring spacious three-bedroom apartments. For those seeking more space, Nakheel Villas and Park Villas provide luxurious villa living within the community.
